How to repurpose YouTube videos?

Short answer: Extract clips, transcribe to text, and transform into platform-native formats.

Full Explanation

Step 1: Identify Key Moments

Watch your video and mark:
  • Quotable statements (15-30 seconds)
  • Teaching moments (30-60 seconds)
  • Hooks and intros (first 3 seconds)
  • Storytelling peaks

Step 2: Extract Short Clips

Create multiple lengths:
  • 15 seconds — TikTok/Reels hooks
  • 30 seconds — Standard short-form
  • 60 seconds — YouTube Shorts, longer Reels

Step 3: Reformat for Platforms

PlatformFormatAspect Ratio

TikTokVertical video9:16 Instagram ReelsVertical video9:16 YouTube ShortsVertical video9:16 Twitter/XHorizontal or square16:9 or 1:1 LinkedInHorizontal16:9

Step 4: Create Text Content

From video transcript:
  • Blog post — Full written version
  • Twitter thread — Key points as tweets
  • Newsletter — Summary + link to full video
  • Quote graphics — Best lines as images

Tools to Use

  • RipurposeAI — Automated multi-platform repurposing
  • Descript — Transcription and editing
  • Canva — Quote graphics

Pro Tips

  • Add captions (85% watch without sound)
  • Use native platform hooks
  • Post clips 1-2 days after full video
  • Link back to full video in descriptions
