What are the best platforms for content creators in 2026?

Short answer: YouTube for income, TikTok for growth, newsletters for ownership.

The Smart Strategy

Don't pick one—pick a system:

1. Discovery platform (TikTok, YouTube Shorts) — Get found 2. Depth platform (YouTube, Podcast) — Build trust 3. Owned platform (Newsletter, Community) — Keep audience

Platform Details

YouTube — Highest RPMs ($3-10 per 1,000 views), search-based discovery, long content lifespan.

TikTok — Unmatched organic reach, younger demographic, algorithm-driven discovery.

Instagram — Best for brand deals, visual-first creators, stories build intimacy.

Podcasting — Most loyal audiences, high CPMs ($25-50), intimate format.

Newsletter — You own the relationship, highest conversion to products, no algorithm.

The Repurposing Advantage

The real answer? Be on multiple platforms efficiently. Create pillar content, then repurpose:

Long podcast → YouTube video → TikTok clips → Newsletter → Twitter thread
