Quick Answer: Influencer marketing is when brands partner with content creators to promote products or services to their audience. It works because followers trust creator recommendations more than traditional ads. The industry exceeds $30 billion annually.

What is influencer marketing?

Short answer: Brands paying creators to promote products to their trusted audiences.

Full Explanation

How It Works

1. Brand identifies creators whose audience matches their target market 2. Brand and creator negotiate terms (fee, deliverables, timeline) 3. Creator produces sponsored content (post, video, story) 4. Creator's audience sees authentic recommendation 5. Brand tracks results (clicks, sales, awareness)

For Creators: Getting Brand Deals

1. Build engaged audience — Quality over quantity 2. Create media kit — Stats, demographics, past work 3. Join platforms — AspireIQ, Grin, Creator.co 4. Pitch directly — Email brands you authentically use 5. Deliver results — Happy brands = repeat deals

The Creator Economy Connection

Influencer marketing fuels the creator economy. More brand spending on creators means more viable creator careers. It's a $30B+ industry growing 15-20% annually.
