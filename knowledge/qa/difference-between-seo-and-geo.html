Quick Answer: SEO optimizes content for traditional search engines like Google, while GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes for AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude that synthesize answers from multiple sources.

What is the difference between SEO and GEO?

Short answer: SEO optimizes for Google's ranked results; GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers.

Full Explanation

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs):
  • Goal: Rank #1 for target keywords
  • Mechanism: Google crawls, indexes, and ranks pages
  • Output: List of blue links
  • Optimization: Keywords, backlinks, technical factors

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

GEO focuses on being cited and referenced by AI systems:
  • Goal: Be the source AI quotes when answering questions
  • Mechanism: AI reads, understands, and synthesizes your content
  • Output: Direct answers that cite your brand
  • Optimization: Structured data, authority signals, machine-readable content
