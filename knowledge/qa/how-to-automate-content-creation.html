How to automate content creation?

Short answer: Use AI for production tasks, scheduling for distribution, and templates for consistency.

Full Explanation

What to Automate

High-value automation:

  • Content repurposing (one → many formats)
  • Transcription and captioning
  • Scheduling and publishing
  • Basic editing and formatting
  • Performance tracking
Keep human:
  • Core ideas and strategy
  • Brand voice and personality
  • Audience relationship
  • Creative direction

Building an Automated Workflow

Step 1: Create pillar content Record one long-form piece (podcast, video, article)

Step 2: Automated transformation AI tools convert to:

  • Short clips (auto-detected highlights)
  • Social posts (key points extracted)
  • Newsletter (summarized)
  • Blog (transcribed and formatted)
Step 3: Scheduled distribution All pieces scheduled across platforms for optimal times

Step 4: Performance tracking Analytics consolidated to identify winners

The 80/20 of Content Automation

Focus automation on:

  • Repetitive formatting tasks
  • Multi-platform distribution
  • Content transformation
This saves 10+ hours/week while maintaining quality.
